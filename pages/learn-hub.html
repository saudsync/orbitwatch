Did You Know?

Quick cosmic facts to spark your curiosity

⚡
Speed of LightLight travels 299,792 km every second — it takes just 1.3 seconds to reach the Moon from Earth.
🌍
Age of EarthEarth is approximately 4.54 billion years old — about one-third the age of the entire universe.
🌟
Stars in Milky WayOur galaxy contains an estimated 100–400 billion stars, many with their own planetary systems.
🕳️
Black Hole MassThe supermassive black hole at our galaxy's center (Sgr A*) is 4 million times the mass of the Sun.
📡
Voyager 1Launched in 1977, Voyager 1 is now over 23 billion km from Earth — the farthest human-made object.
🌌
Observable UniverseThe observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter and contains ~2 trillion galaxies.
