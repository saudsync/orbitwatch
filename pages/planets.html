PLANET 01 — INNERMOST
Mercury
Terrestrial · Rocky
ROCKY PLANET

The smallest and fastest planet, Mercury races around the Sun in just 88 days. Despite its proximity to the Sun, it has no atmosphere to retain heat — temperatures swing from −180°C at night to 430°C during the day.

ROCKY PLANET

Earth
Terrestrial · Ocean World
HABITABLE WORLD

The only known planet harboring life. Earth's perfect conditions — liquid water, protective atmosphere, strong magnetic field, and distance from the Sun — make it uniquely suited for complex biology.

ROCKY PLANET

Gas Giant · Jovian
GAS GIANT

The largest planet — so massive it contains more than twice the mass of all other planets combined. Its Great Red Spot is a storm that has raged for over 350 years. Acts as the solar system's gravitational shield.

GAS GIANT

The strangest planet — Uranus rotates on its side with an axial tilt of 98°, likely caused by a massive ancient collision. Its blue-green color comes from methane in its icy atmosphere.
